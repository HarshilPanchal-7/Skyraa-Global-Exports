Our Story

Founded in 2005, Skyraa Global Export began with a simple vision: to share the authentic flavors of India with the world. What started as a small trading operation has grown into a comprehensive export enterprise with our own production facilities and farmhouses.

Our journey has been marked by an unwavering commitment to quality, transparency, and building lasting relationships with our global partners. Today, we serve clients across 25+ countries, spanning Europe, CIS nations, and the Asia-Pacific region.

With nearly two decades of experience, we understand the nuances of international trade, quality requirements, and the importance of timely delivery. Our team combines traditional knowledge of Indian spices with modern quality assurance practices.
